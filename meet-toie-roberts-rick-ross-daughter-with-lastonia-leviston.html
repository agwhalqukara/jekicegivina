Meet Toie Roberts, Rick Ross Daughter with Lastonia Leviston

Toie gained some public attention due to her father’s fame and success in the music industry, but besides the popularity she has gained as a celebrity kid, Toie has made a name for herself as a social media sensation and an entrepreneur.

She owns a clothing line and uses her social media platform to promote and market her brand. Learn other interesting facts about Toie Roberts below.

Who Is Toie Roberts?

Toie Roberts was born on March 17, 2002, to American rapper and record executive William Leonard Roberts II, popularly known as Rick Ross, and his ex-partner, Lastonia Leviston. There are no details of what her early childhood looked like or what school she attended. However, it is assumed to have been filled with so much love as her parents dearly love her.

She is not the rapper’s only child. However, she is his only child with Lastonia Leviston, and her rapper father spends lavishly on her. On her 13th birthday, Rick Ross booked Ace Hood, K Camp, and Rich Homie Quan to perform at her party. Toie has three half-siblings from her father’s relationships with other women. They are William Roberts III, Berkeley Hermes, and Billionaire Heir.

Meet Toie Roberts’ Famous Parents

Rick Ross

Toie’s parents are not people unfamiliar to many. Her father, William Leonard Roberts II, popularly known as Rick Ross, is one US rapper that well recognized across the globe. Before Rick got into music, he worked in Florida as a correctional officer and went on tour with Southern rapper Trick Daddy.

Until he signed a multi-million-dollar deal with Jay-Z on Def Jam Recordings in 2006, Rick was the subject of a bidding war, receiving offers from Irv Gotti’s Murder Inc and Diddy’s Bad Boy Entertainment. His first studio album, Port of Miami, hit the number 1 spot on the Billboard 200 in 2006, and so did his second studio Trilla which came two years later.

So far, the rapper has dropped eleven studio albums. In 2009, he founded a record label named Maybach Music Group, and in  2012, MTV named him the Hottest MC in the Game. Rick Ross is named among the richest rappers in the US, with an estimated net worth of $45 million.

Lastonia Leviston is Toie Roberts’s Mom

Although Lastonia may not be as famous as her ex-partner Rick, her name does ring a bell each time it is mentioned. Lastonia was the woman who slammed the rapper 50 Cent in a lawsuit for publicly publishing her sex tape on his website. The sex tape was one she made with her then-boyfriend, Maurice Murray, back in 2009. 50 Cent allegedly bought the sex tape from Murray afterward, doctored, and uploaded it on his website with his commentary.

 Leviston said the tape destroyed her life and pushed her to contemplate suicide; thus, she sued him for violating her civil rights and inflicting emotional pain on her. Although many saw it as 50 Cent’s way of getting back at Rick, with whom he has a long-standing feud and rivalry, Leviston’s lawyers thought otherwise.

They claimed that the rapper used the video to drive traffic to his website and increase his own business. After the lawsuit, she was awarded a $5 million compensation. She had remained private ever since, except when she appeared in her daughter’s pictures and recently when she posted about Toie expecting a baby.

Lastonia Leviston and Rick Ross’s Untold Love Story

Well, it is not clear if Leviston and Rick had a love story as there is no information about how the duo met and how their relationship was all the time they were a couple. According to sources, they were together for roughly two years (2000-2002) and broke up the same year they welcomed their daughter.

The reason for their separation is unknown. Although no longer an item, Rick and  Leviston have done well parenting their now-adult daughter.

Rick Ross’ Daughter Has Her Clothing And Cosmetics Line

The  21-year-old has her clothing and cosmetics line. She is the CEO of a clothing line called Four Two Clothing and a cosmetics line Ashanti Beauty.

On the clothing line website fourtwobrnd.com, she sells Rompers, jumpsuits, dresses, and lots more. While on the Ashanti Beauty IG page, she sells hair extensions, beauty products, and accessories.

She is a Social Media Influencer

Besides being a successful entrepreneur, Toie has paved the way for herself as a social media sensation. She has almost 275,000 followers on Instagram and nearly 100,000 more on TikTok, where she often posts outfit shots and makeup looks.

She once had an IG account @mmg_princess with large followership. Unfortunately, it was deactivated, and she had to start building again from scratch. This may explain why she has little content on her current IG Instagram.

Toie Roberts Shot Down Pregnancy Rumors In 2019

In September 2019, rumors began that Toie was pregnant with her first child. This was after Baby Gaga reported that the influencer’s mother took to her social media page to congratulate her expectant baby with a photo of her daughter’s baby bump.

After the news, reports had it that Ross wasn’t happy that his little girl was soon to be a mother and went ahead to unfollow her on IG and delete photos of her from his Instagram page. A few weeks later, Toie Roberts down rumors that she is pregnant by posting a photo on Instagram showing off her flat belly. She wrote, “I’m not even pregnant, and my child is still richer than u. u lose.”
