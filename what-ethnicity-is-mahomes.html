What ethnicity is Mahomes?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Patrick Lavon Mahomes II is an American football quarterback for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League (NFL). The son of former Major League Baseball (MLB) pitcher Pat Mahomes, he initially played college football and college baseball at Texas Tech University. He was selected 10th overall by the Kansas City Chiefs in the 2017 NFL Draft.

Mahomes was awarded the Super Bowl MVP for his performance, the second African-American quarterback and youngest overall to do so. He is also the third African American quarterback to win a Super Bowl.
