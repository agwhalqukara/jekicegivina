Behati Prinsloo Flashes Wedding Ring Tattoo on Left Hand: Photo

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 24, 2024

He liked it, so she put a tattoo on it!

Although she loves her vintage diamond engagement ring from hubby Adam Levine, Behati Prinsloo nixes it in her day-to-day life. Instead, the Namibian model, 25, is rocking some special ink on her ring finger.

“Shocker! Everyone’s going to talk about it,” the new Mrs. Levine, 25, jokingly gasped to Yahoo! Style. “I wanted a ring [tattoo], but everybody does the ring. So I did three dots.”

Prinsloo, who tied the knot with Levine in Mexico last July, explained the tiny tattoo during the video chat. “It means being one with the earth,” the model, who recently costarred in her husband’s music video for “Animals,” said. “And I just wanted it on my ring finger so when I take [my engagement ring] off, it’s still there!”

The Victoria’s Secret Angel—who’s busy prepping for the #VSFashionShow, airing Dec. 9—is just the latest to take part in the Hollywood trend: Beyonce has the Roman numerals IV tatted on her engagement ring finger to honor her husband, Jay Z, while Dax Shepard had a bell inked onto his hand for his wife, Kristen Bell.
