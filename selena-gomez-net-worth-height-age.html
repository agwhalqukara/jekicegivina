Selena Gomez net worth, height, age

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Selena Gomez was born on July 22, 1992, in Grand Prairie, Texas, to Ricardo Joel Gomez and Texas-born former stage actress Mandy Teefey. She is an American singer, actress, and producer.

Selena Gomez first movie role

Gomez began her acting career on the children’s television series Barney & Friends (2002–2004).

Selena Gomez movies

Gomez has appeared in productions like Another Cinderella Story (2008), Wizards of Waverly Place: The Movie (2009), Ramona and Beezus (2010), Monte Carlo (2011), Spring Breakers (2012), Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising (2016), and The Dead Don’t Die (2019), and voiced Mavis in the Hotel Transylvania film franchise (2012–2022).

Selena Gomez age

She was born on July 22, 1992- hence she is 30 years old.

Selena Gomez biography

She was named after Tejano singer Selena Quintanilla, who died in 1995.

In the 1970s, Gomez’s paternal grandparents moved from Monterrey to Texas. Gomez has described herself as “a proud third-generation American-Mexican” in regard to her ancestry. and “We attend the communion church, and my family does celebrate quinceaneras. We practice everything Catholic, but aside from Sunday barbecues in the park, we don’t really follow any traditional customs.” Until the age of seven, Gomez spoke Spanish with ease.

When she was five years old, her parents got a divorce, and she stayed with her mother. Gomez has three younger siblings: Victoria “Tori” and Marcus Gomez through Ricardo and his second wife, Sara, and Gracie Elliot Teefey and Brian Teefey through Mandy and her second husband, Brian Teefey. In May 2010, she completed her homeschooling and received her high school diploma.

Gomez was born when her mother was 16 years old. The family had financial troubles throughout Gomez’s childhood, with her mother struggling to provide for the pair. At one point, Gomez recalls that they had to search for quarters just to get gas for their car.

Selena Gomez net worth

She has a net worth of $85 million.

Selena Gomez height
