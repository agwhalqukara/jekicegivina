Lovely Mimi’s Family and Early Life

Lovely Mimi was born in Vietnam on August 20, 1990. She had a tough childhood because she spent the majority of it in a refugee camp. Her parents fled from the camp and settled in Silver Springs, California.

Her father was a priest in Vietnam until his death in 2014. She is the youngest of six siblings, with five sisters and one brother. Her eldest sister lives in Vietnam, and she has only seen her once in her life.

Mimi is married to her long-term boyfriend Remy, and they have two children, Juice and Jayy. Her children are becoming Instagram celebrities through their joint account, JuicenJayy, which is managed by their parents.

Lovely Mimi’s Career & Net Worth from Instagram IG and TikTok

Mimi spent the majority of her life in juvenile facilities as a result of her drinking and rebellious nature. She did not finish her schooling. She bought a nail art salon in Capitol Heights, Maryland, and called it Luong’s Lovely Nails; this is how her career began and progressed.

Mimi signed up for Instagram under the handle @mimilovelynails. She shared a number of photographs and videos of her salon. Recognizing the salon’s sluggish growth, she opened a new account and called it @itslovelymimi. She used the page to teach people how to paint their nails and apply make-up. Snoop Dogg and Dabrat, both celebrities, shared her tweets, and the response was overwhelming. Her brilliant sense of humor attracted a large number of viewers to watch her videos online.

One blogger dubbed her the “ratchet Asian teen,” which drew the attention of Nick Cannon, the Wild ‘n Out host on MTV. Mimi first appeared on the show in 2017, in the second episode of the sixth season. She moved to Atlanta and became a regular on the comedy show. She also purchased a nail salon with employees and called it UltraViolet Nail Lounge. Her business has expanded tremendously, and she is now regarded as the best nail artist in the entire world. She owns five salons in the Atlanta area.

Lovely Mimi Height, Weight, and Measurements

Lovely Mimi Net Worth

Lovely Mimi’s net worth is projected to be $2 million as of 2021. Her salon is her primary source of profits. She makes a decent living as an actress and comedian on the reality show. She still makes money via her Instagram account and YouTube channel.
