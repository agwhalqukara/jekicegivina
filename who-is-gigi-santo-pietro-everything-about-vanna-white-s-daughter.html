Who Is Gigi Santo Pietro? Everything About Vanna Whites Daughter

Guess who is Gigi Santo Pietro, a star kid. She is also among those kids who got fame and name without doing anything because they are star kids.

The same goes with Gigi Santo; she is the daughter of Pietro, American TV host Vanna White and well-known Italian-American restaurateur, George Santo Pietro.

Whose given name is Giovanna Santo Pietro. Gigi, now 25 years old, became a celebrity immediately after birth, thanks to her renowned parents.

Gigi is a fully-formed adult who has settled on a specific career path. Sure, but what? Even though she’s a famous actor’s kid, many mysteries surrounding her life have yet to be revealed. Thus, here are all details related to Gigi Santo Pietro;

Education

Regarding Gigi’s education, it’s important to note that she received her diploma from the Tisch School of the Arts at New York University. There is currently no other information about her education that has been made public. 

Nikko, her brother, attended and graduated from Oregon State University. Additionally, she revealed in Closer Weekly that the young kid of Vanna had participated in the University of Arizona at one time in his life.

Profession & Tattoo Artist

Ever since she was a child, Gigi has had a strong interest in the creative arts. She is pursuing her schooling in the same manner that she is pursuing her interests, and at the same time, she is attempting to create a career in the arts.

Her bio on Instagram states that she works as an apprentice at Transition Tattoo Studio, located in Huntington Beach, California.

Gigi’s interests extend beyond tattooing to include photography and drawing; however, she has not yet produced significant work in any of these areas despite her enthusiasm for them.

Gigi Santo Pietro Mother, Vanna White

Vanna Marie Rosich is Gigi mother.  She began her professional life in the modeling industry in 1978, when she entered several beauty pageants and competed for the title of Miss Georgia.

She was invited to become the co-hostess of the game show “Wheel of Fortune” in October 1982, when the previous hostess, Susan Stafford, quit the show. It followed her sporadic appearances among the contestants on the show “The Price Is Right.”

Vanna’s heart was shattered when the dancer and actor John Gibson passed away in an accident in 1985. The couple had been dating during the 1980s, and Vanna was already engaged to him.

In the end, she tied the knot with George Santo Pietro in 1989, and the couple went on to have two children together: Nicholas and Giovanna.

Gigi Santo Pietro has a strong bond with her mother throughout her life.

Gigi Santo Pietro Father, George Santo Pietro

George Santo Pietro, Gigi’s father, had a significant interest in the business from an early age, and as a result, he began working while he was still a teenager.

He eventually created a successful career as a restaurateur, real estate developer and manager, and property manager.

Between 1997 and 2003, he participated in several film productions in which he had the role of dolly grip. He is a businessman and filmmaker.

After spending more than a decade together as a married couple, Vanna and George finalized their divorce in 2002. Later, he tied the knot with Melissa Mascari, and she became pregnant with their child.

It was the third child for George and the first child for Melissa. George’s relationship with his older children is currently stable and amicable. He is not living with Gigi and her mother.

Gigi Santo Pietro Sibling, Nikko Santo Pietro

Gigi Santa Pietro is the younger of two children older is Nikko Santo Pietro.  While Nikko is 27 years old, having been born on June 10, 1994. She shares a tight relationship, and Nikko is like a second father to her.

Gigi Santo Pietro passion of Traveling and Art
