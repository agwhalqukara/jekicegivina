FAQs about Aidy Bryant

Who is Aidy Bryant?

She is an American actress and comedian, known for her 10-season stint on Saturday Night Live (SNL) from 2012 to 2022. She’s celebrated for her versatility and comedic timing.

What has Bryant done since leaving SNL?

After SNL, Aidy has been quite busy. She starred in the Hulu series Shrill and took on voice acting roles in the animated series Human Resources. Additionally, she’s been involved in directing a music video and is developing a new series titled Lonely Heart’s Club.

Has Bryant won any awards?

While she hasn’t clinched an Emmy yet, she has received several nominations for her work, particularly from her time on SNL and her role in Shrill.

What is Bryant’s background in comedy?

Aidy’s comedic journey began with the musical improv group Baby Wants Candy and continued with her work at The Second City, which eventually led to her role on SNL.

Is Aidy Bryant married?

Yes, she is married to Conner O’Malley, a fellow comedian and writer whom she met while both were performing at The Second City in Chicago.

What are some of Bryant’s most notable roles?

Besides SNL, Aidy is known for her lead role in Shrill, and her voice roles in Danger & Eggs and Human Resources. She also appeared in films like The Big Sick and I Feel Pretty.
