Potcoin Price: What Is The Future Of This Coin?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

The Potcoin cryptocurrency has evolved into a remarkable phenomenon because its payment system is designed to make payments in the legalized cannabis industry. The POT cryptocurrency was developed in 2014. 

At that time, recreational marijuana kiosks appeared and operated in the western United States. Thus, this cryptocurrency is intended to pay for purchases.

Potcoin is a digital currency that permits consumers to buy and sell cannabis products anonymously. Potcoin was conceived of as a bitcoin that is used to facilitate transactions within the burgeoning legal cannabis industry.

Potcoin was founded in January 2014 by three devotees online with the pseudonyms Hasoshi, Mr. Jones, and Smokemon 514. 

The cryptocurrency was designed to fill a gap in the legal marijuana industry where regulators and financial institutions were slow in adapting to the economic change that U.S. states were bringing about by legalizing marijuana.
