Gwen Lockman Wiki: Age & Family Life

Lockman is 28 years old now. She was born Gwendolyn R Lockman on 13 September 1994 in Poplar, Montana, United States.

At age five, she moved with her family to Missoula, Montana. Her parents are Clemance Lockman and Jamie Lockman. Clemance is a Certified Public Accountant and Jamie is a Proposal Development Analyst at the University of Montana.

Gwen Lockman pictured with her parents. (Source: Clemance’s Facebook)

Gwen attended Sentinel High School in her hometown, graduating in 2012. She then went to Georgetown University and graduated Magna Cum Laude with a degree in American Studies in 2016.

Moreover, she earned her Master of Arts in History from The University of Texas at Austin in 2020. Currently, she is pursuing her Ph.D. in American History at the same university.

Gwen Lockman Husband Or Boyfriend: Is She Married Now?

Lockman is unmarried and does not have a husband. However, she is off the market now.

The 28-year-old is dating a handsome boyfriend as per her Facebook posts. Her boyfriend is also a Montana native and a scientist. The duo seems to be together for several years.

Gwen Lockman has not shared details about her boyfriend. (Source: Facebook)

Gwen has shared some pictures of enjoying wonderful moments with her boyfriend on her Facebook account. She also has a strong presence on Twitter and Instagram.

What Is Gwen Lockman’s Job?

Lockman now holds a job as a Junior Fellow in Garden and Landscape Studies at Dumbarton Oaks. She held a job in various sectors as an internee.

While at college, she worked in the legal department for the MLB team, Washington Nationals Baseball Club. Likewise, she served as an office manager for the MLB Event Operations Headquarters during the 2018 MLB All-Star Game.
